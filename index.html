About Me

I am an early years teacher with experience supporting young multilingual learners in international classrooms in Asia. My teaching focuses on structure, warmth, movement, language development, phonics, and practical classroom routines that help children feel safe enough to take risks and grow.

Teaching Philosophy

I believe young learners need clear routines, meaningful language practice, movement, encouragement, and repeated chances to succeed. My goal is to build classrooms where students feel known, supported, and challenged in ways that match their developmental needs.
